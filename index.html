About Mathodic
Where equations become music (and homework becomes bearable)
Mathodic is a deterministic math-to-music engine — every character in your expression maps to a unique musical event. Digits hit scale degrees, operators shift register and rhythm, functions weave arpeggios, and = resolves into a full cadence chord. Same input, same tune, every time. Change one digit, and it'll let you know.

No AI wrote this melody for you. It's just math, doing what math does best: being weirdly musical about everything.
